## ðŸŽ¯ Sales Process

### **Lead Qualification**
**BANT Criteria**:
- **Budget**: â‚¬50-500/month
- **Authority**: Decision maker or influencer
- **Need**: Property management challenges
- **Timeline**: 3-6 months

**Qualification Questions**:
1. How many apartments do you manage?
2. What's your current property management process?
3. What are your biggest challenges?
4. Who's involved in the decision process?
5. What's your timeline for implementation?

### **Sales Process**
**Step 1: Discovery (Week 1)**
- **Qualify lead** using BANT criteria
- **Understand needs** and challenges
- **Identify decision makers** and timeline
- **Schedule demo** if qualified

**Step 2: Demo (Week 2)**
- **Personalized demo** based on needs
- **Show relevant features** and benefits
- **Address objections** and concerns
- **Next steps** and timeline

**Step 3: Proposal (Week 3)**
- **Customized proposal** with pricing
- **Implementation timeline** and support
- **ROI calculation** and benefits
- **Contract terms** and conditions

**Step 4: Close (Week 4)**
- **Follow up** on proposal
- **Address final objections**
- **Negotiate terms** if needed
- **Close deal** and onboard
